MapToCar

Save places fast. Launch any route.

Version 1.0.0

Click any image to view it full size.

Description

MapToCar helps you collect, organize, and launch destinations quickly.

Save places from in-app search, Apple Maps, Google Maps, shared links, or manual entry.

Organize places with custom lists and pinned locations, then open routes in Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze.

Import saved locations from Google Maps Takeout (GeoJSON) and Apple Maps Guides, and browse everything from a search-first world map view.

1.0.0 Key Features

  • Save places from in-app search, Apple Maps, Google Maps, shared links, or manual entry
  • Organize destinations with custom lists and pinned locations
  • Open destinations in Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze, or share destination details to your car app
  • Import saved places from Google Maps Takeout (GeoJSON) and Apple Maps Guides
  • Explore all saved places on a world map with a clean, search-first workflow
  • Privacy-first design with all place data stored locally on device
