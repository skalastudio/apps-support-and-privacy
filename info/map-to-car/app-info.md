# Apple App Review Info

## EN

### Promotional Text
Save places in seconds, organize them with lists and pins, and send destinations to your car or navigation apps. Import from Google Maps and Apple Maps.

### Description
Map to Car helps you collect, organize, and launch destinations fast.

- Save places from in-app search, Apple Maps, Google Maps, shared links, or manual entry.
- Organize your places with custom lists and pinned locations for quick access.
- Open destinations in Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze, or share destination details to your car app.
- Import saved places from Google Maps Takeout (GeoJSON) and import Apple Maps Guides.
- View your saved places on a world map and access them from a clean search-first workflow.
- Privacy-first design: your place data is stored on device.

### Keywords
